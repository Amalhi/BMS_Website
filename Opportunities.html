Get Involved

Want to become a Board Member, sponsor, Yell or Junior Coach?

Board Member

Board Members: Board members are simply people who want to help and direct Burnaby MoreSports. You don’t need to have any special skills like accounting or a master’s degree. If you want to help make decisions to keep the organization running, join the Board of Burnaby MoreSports. All we need is your time and enthusiasm.



Sponsor

Sponsors: Burnaby MoreSports is always looking for individuals or companies to help us help kids play sports. We accept donations of healthy snacks, sport goods and, of course, money. If you think you can help out in any way, please contact us to discuss.

Junior Coach

Junior Coach: The Junior coach program is designed to get Grade 6 and 7 students involved. Typically these kids have participated in Burnaby MoreSports programs and want to continue to be involved. As Junior Coaches, they get to help run the program they previously participated in. While the program is not as formal as the YELL coach program, Junior Coaches will get experience coaching kids in a supportive environment.

For more information on any of our programs, please contact us using the information on our Contact Us page.

YELL

Yell Coach: YELL (Youth Engage Learn Lead), is about gaining leadership skills in a hands-on sports context. You will receive training and certifications, but much of the learning will happen through your volunteer work in the Burnaby MoreSports program. Volunteers are a huge part of our program – we simply couldn’t do it without the positive energy and commitment of the YELL coaches.

All YELL participants receive training and certifications, free of charge. Through your training and volunteer experience you will learn to work with children, lead recreational sport programs, and practice other valuable skills in a safe and supported environment. The YELL Leadership Program can also help you gain valuable work experience for resumes, meet graduation requirements, and receive reference letters. We hope you will even have some fun in the process!
