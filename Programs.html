What does Burnaby MoreSports offer...

Burnaby MoreSports currently runs in three locations:
Edmonds Community school, Morley Elementary School and Stride Community School.

Burnaby MoreSports is offered on the following schedule:

Edmonds Community School

Tuesday’s and Thursday’s

Grade 2 and 3: 3:00 – 4:00pm
Grade 4 and 5: 4:00 – 5:00pm

Morley Elementary School

Monday’s and Wednesday’s

Grade 2 and 3: 3:00 – 4:00pm
Grade 4 and 5: 4:00 – 5:00pm

Stride Elementary School

Tuesday’s and Thursday’s

Grade 2 and 3: 3:00 – 4:00pm
Grade 4 and 5: 4:00 – 5:00pm

Leaders of tomorrow- Junior Coaches and YELL

One of the cornerstones to Burnaby MoreSports is the youth leadership development program for grade 10 and 11 students. This leadership program includes skills for being a coach and the High 5 – Principles of Healthy Child Development course. These young leaders then commit to volunteering their time to coach the elementary students.

Partners

MoreSports is made possible by the commitment and dedication of the community partners and sponsors who help sustain the delivery and growth of the MoreSports program. The partners for Burnaby MoreSports include Burnaby School District, Burnaby Parks, Recreation and Cultural Services and South Burnaby Neighborhood House.

Burnaby MoreSports welcomes new partners, volunteers, and sponsors to join. Whether your commitment is financial, in kind or the gift of your time, it is a key factor in sustaining and building the program.
